Circular Economy Workshop - India

Bridging traditional wisdom and modern sustainability in the heart of India.

Workshop Overview

This special edition of our business workshop series was held in India, focusing on the principles of the Circular Economy. We worked with students to analyze local waste streams and develop business concepts that turn "waste" into value. By combining modern economic theories with India's rich history of resourcefulness, students created innovative solutions for their local communities.

Curriculum & Activities

Target Audience

High School Students

Duration

1 Day Intensive (6 Hours)

Key Topics

  • Circular Economy
  • Social Entrepreneurship
  • Green Marketing
  • Impact Investing

Workshop Goals

By the end of this workshop, students will be able to evaluate business models for sustainability, understand the importance of stakeholder engagement, and pitch a basic sustainable business concept.

Agenda

Session 1: The Triple Bottom Line (1.5 Hours)

Introduction to sustainable business concepts. Case studies of successful purpose-driven companies.

Session 2: Circular Design Challenge (1.5 Hours)

Hands-on activity where groups redesign a common product to be zero-waste and circular.

Session 3: Ethical Marketing & Branding (1.5 Hours)

Learning how to communicate impact authentically and avoid greenwashing.

Session 4: Pitch Competition (1.5 Hours)

Teams develop and present a sustainable startup idea to a panel of local business leaders.
