Business Initiatives

Fostering Sustainable Entrepreneurship

Our Business Initiatives prepare students to become sustainable entrepreneurs and business leaders. We provide workshops, mentorship programs, and resources that teach young people how to build businesses that prioritize environmental responsibility and social impact.

💼

Business Workshops

Comprehensive workshops covering sustainable business models, green marketing, and ethical entrepreneurship.

🤝

Mentorship Programs

Connecting students with experienced business leaders who share insights on sustainable business practices.

📈

Startup Support

Providing resources and guidance for students interested in launching their own sustainable ventures.

🌍

Impact Measurement

Teaching students how to measure and communicate the environmental and social impact of their business ideas.
